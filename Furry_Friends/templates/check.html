AI helps you analyze X-ray images !

You can get help from AI when you want to make a diagnosis through abdominal, chest, or musculoskeletal X-ray images of dogs and cats. Quick auxiliary diagnosis using AI can increase the accuracy and speed of diagnosis. The model accumulates data to update the model and can be used as a learning resource. The model has 85% accuracy and 80% sensitivity.
